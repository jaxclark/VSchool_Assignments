ABOUT US

Jax's Bromeliads was founded in 2019 out of a love for these beautiful plants, and a desire to share that love with others. We started the nursery as a hobby because of our love for these tropical plants, but as our collection grew and others in the hobby started seeing this extensive collection, interest started growing in acquiring some of our unique plants. Our goals now are to :

  • Teach others about environmentally sound gardening practices
  • Create new and healthy bromeliads for fellow collectors and plant lovers
  • Spread knowledge of proper care for these easy household plants
